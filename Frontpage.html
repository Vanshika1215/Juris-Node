Our platform processes complex legal instruments — from ITAR schedules and FAR clauses to HIPAA data-sharing agreements — and returns structured risk intelligence that helps you act fast without cutting corners.

Traffic Light Risk Scoring

PDF Ingestion Pipeline

Drop any text-selectable or OCR-scanned federal contract, policy instrument, or compliance document. Our extraction engine isolates relevant clauses with high precision.

Donut and pie chart visualizations give you an instant quantitative breakdown of clause risk distribution — exportable as part of a full assessment report.

Executive Summary Engine

Process

Four steps to clarity.

01
Upload Document

Mount your legal instrument — federal contract, compliance schedule, or policy framework — directly into the ingestion gateway.

02
Clause Extraction

The pipeline isolates every actionable clause, cross-referencing against 40+ regulatory frameworks including FAR, ITAR, HIPAA, and FedRAMP.

03
Risk Classification

Each clause receives a traffic-light rating — Critical, Caution, or Standard — with a plain-language rationale and the implicated statute cited.

04
Report & Archive

Review your aggregated safety score, risk charts, and executive summary. Print the full assessment report or retrieve it later from the archive.

⚠️

Juris Node outputs constitute AI-assisted risk analysis and data visualization — not direct statutory legal counsel. Always consult a licensed attorney for formal legal advice.
